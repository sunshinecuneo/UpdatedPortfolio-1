About Me

I am a single mom to a wonderfully precocious, very happy nine year old whose name is Grace! We have a smiley dog named Mavis and three chickens; Murdoch, April and Little Peepers. One of our chickens just died which I tried nursing back to health for ten days. Wa Wa! I live in Denver in the house I grew up in. I am a fiercely indepedant introvert who loves most to be at home enjoying time with my daughter and our little urban farm family. I enjoy sleeping, watching tv, gardening and cooking and baking, especially for others. I can fix or construct just about anything. I have a very strong intuition that I trust completely.
